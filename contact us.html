Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

Our regular school hours Mondays to Thursdays are from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM while Fridays are from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM for all students. Early care is available from 7:00 AM and after-school programs run until 3:00 PM.

Do you offer transportation services?

No, we don't offer transportation services to various locations throughout Kaduna.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention. Our ratios are: Day Care - 1:5, Nursery - 1:10, Primary - 1:15, Secondary - 1:20.

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships to less priviledged children. Please contact our admissions office for more information and application details.
