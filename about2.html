About Green Sprouts Academy

Discover our legacy of educational excellence since 2021

Who We Are

Green Sprouts Academy is a premier educational institution dedicated to fostering intellectual growth, creativity, and character development in a diverse community of learners. Our holistic approach to education prepares students for success in college and beyond.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, we provide an environment where students can explore their passions, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible global citizens.

Founded in 2021, we've been at the forefront of innovative education for over four years, adapting to the changing needs of our students while maintaining our core values of excellence, integrity, and community.

Our Campus Life

Our Vision & Mission

At Green Sprouts Academy, we envision a world where every student develops the knowledge, skills, and character to make a positive impact in their community and the world.

Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where students achieve academic excellence while developing character and leadership skills. We are committed to creating lifelong learners who are critical thinkers, problem solvers, and compassionate leaders.

Our Leadership

Mrs. Silifat Sulaiman

Proprietress and Director

With over 25 years of experience in education, Mrs. Sulaiman founded Green Sprouts Academy with a vision to create an institution that balances academic rigor with character development. Her leadership has guided the school to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Mrs. Sulaiman holds a Master's degree in Educational Leadership and is passionate about creating inclusive learning environments where every child can thrive. Under her guidance, GSA has developed innovative programs that have been recognized at both national and international levels.
