About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence Since 2021

Green Sprouts Academy is a premier educational institution dedicated to fostering intellectual growth, creativity, and character development in a diverse community of learners. Our holistic approach to education prepares students for success in college and beyond.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty, we provide an environment where students can explore their passions, develop critical thinking skills, and become responsible global citizens.
