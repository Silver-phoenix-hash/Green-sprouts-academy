Our Curriculum Approach

At Green Sprouts Academy, we follow a holistic curriculum that balances academic excellence with character development and practical life skills. Our approach includes:

  • Integrated STEM education with hands-on learning experiences
  • Language arts program emphasizing communication and critical thinking
  • Social studies curriculum focused on global citizenship and cultural awareness
  • Arts education including visual arts, music, and performing arts
  • Physical education promoting health, wellness, and teamwork
  • Character education program fostering integrity, responsibility, and empathy
  • Technology integration across all subject areas
  • Project-based learning opportunities
